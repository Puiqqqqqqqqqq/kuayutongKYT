The charging case can provide an additional 16 hours of battery life, with a total battery life of up to 24 hours.

The headphone supports fast charging function, charging for 15 minutes can be used for 2 hours. IPX5 waterproof rating design, suitable for use during exercise.

2. Features

  • Bluetooth 5.0 technology, stable connection
  • High-definition sound quality, supporting stereo
  • Touch control, easy operation
  • Voice assistant compatible (Siri, Google Assistant)
  • IPX5 waterproof rating
